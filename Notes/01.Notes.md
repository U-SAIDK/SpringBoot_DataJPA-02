# рҹ’Ў Golden Rules Followed Throughout This Handbook

### Rule 1

Understand the concept before memorizing annotations.

---

### Rule 2

Every annotation exists to solve a problem.

Always understand **why** it is used before learning **how** to use it.

---

### Rule 3

Focus on production-ready practices.

Avoid shortcuts or techniques that are commonly discouraged in real-world applications.

---

### Rule 4

Prefer understanding over memorization.

Knowing how Hibernate works internally is more valuable than remembering dozens of annotations.

---

### Rule 5

Always connect theory with code.

Every concept should relate back to the **SpringBoot-JPA** project.
